Finasteride 0.5% & Minoxidil 5% Foam Information

What is Finasteride & Minoxidil Foam?

This is a combination topical treatment for male pattern hair loss. Finasteride 0.5% works internally to block DHT, a hormone responsible for hair loss, while Minoxidil 5% stimulates hair growth and improves scalp blood flow.

How It Works

Minoxidil increases blood flow to hair follicles and prolongs the growth phase, while Finasteride reduces DHT levels, preventing further hair thinning. Together they promote hair regrowth and slow hair loss progression.

Common Uses

  • Male pattern baldness
  • Hair thinning prevention
  • Hair regrowth stimulation

Dosage

Apply Minoxidil 5% foam once daily to the affected scalp areas. Finasteride 0.5% is typically taken orally or as prescribed. Follow your healthcare provider’s guidance for best results.

Side Effects

  • Scalp irritation (minoxidil)
  • Dryness or itching
  • Headache
  • Sexual side effects (finasteride)
  • Dizziness (rare)

Warnings

Do not use if allergic to minoxidil or finasteride. Women who are pregnant or may become pregnant should avoid contact with the foam. Always follow your healthcare provider’s instructions.
