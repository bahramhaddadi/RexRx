Sildenafil (Viagra) Information

What is Sildenafil?

Sildenafil is a prescription medication used to treat erectile dysfunction (ED) by improving blood flow to the penis. It is also used under different brand names for pulmonary arterial hypertension.

How It Works

Sildenafil is a PDE5 inhibitor that relaxes blood vessels and increases blood flow during sexual stimulation, helping achieve and maintain an erection.

Common Uses

  • Erectile Dysfunction (ED)
  • Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension (under medical supervision)

Dosage

Sildenafil is commonly available in 25 mg, 50 mg, and 100 mg tablets. It is typically taken as needed about 30–60 minutes before sexual activity.

Side Effects

  • Headache
  • Flushing
  • Upset stomach
  • Vision changes (rare)
  • Nasal congestion

Warnings

Do not use sildenafil with nitrates or certain heart medications. Consult your healthcare provider if you have heart conditions, low blood pressure, or are taking other medications.
