Mounjaro (Tirzepatide) Information

What is Mounjaro?

Mounjaro is a prescription medication containing tirzepatide, used to improve blood sugar control in adults with type 2 diabetes. It is also increasingly prescribed for weight loss under medical supervision.

How It Works

Mounjaro is a dual GIP and GLP-1 receptor agonist that helps regulate blood sugar, reduce appetite, and improve metabolic function, leading to significant weight loss and better glucose control.

Common Uses

  • Type 2 Diabetes Management
  • Weight Loss (under medical supervision)
  • Improving metabolic health

Dosage

Mounjaro is administered as a once-weekly injection. Treatment typically starts at 2.5 mg and is gradually increased based on response and tolerability.

Side Effects

  • Nausea
  • Diarrhea
  • Decreased appetite
  • Vomiting
  • Constipation

Warnings

Mounjaro may not be suitable for individuals with a history of thyroid cancer or certain endocrine disorders. Always consult a healthcare provider before starting treatment.
