Wegovy (Semaglutide) Information

What is Wegovy?

Wegovy is a prescription medication containing semaglutide, specifically approved for chronic weight management in adults with obesity or overweight conditions with related health risks.

How It Works

Wegovy is a GLP-1 receptor agonist that helps regulate appetite, reduce food intake, and slow gastric emptying, leading to sustained weight loss over time.

Common Uses

  • Chronic weight management
  • Obesity treatment
  • Weight-related health risk reduction

Dosage

Wegovy is administered as a once-weekly injection. The dose is gradually increased over several weeks, typically reaching a maintenance dose of 2.4 mg weekly.

Side Effects

  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Constipation
  • Abdominal discomfort

Warnings

Wegovy may not be suitable for individuals with a history of thyroid cancer or certain endocrine disorders. Always consult a healthcare provider before starting treatment.
